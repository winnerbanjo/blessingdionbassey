About

Meet Blessing Bassey

Blessing Bassey is a Nigerian fashion designer, entrepreneur, and educator. Since launching Dion Baci in 2017, her work has grown into a multi-dimensional fashion house spanning bridal couture, ready-to-wear, and fashion education.

Her world spans Dion Baci couture, refined ready-to-wear through Baci, and everyday essentials through Bacibaci.
